01 — Initial discussion

We learn what you need
before we price anything.

We take time to understand your portfolio, LP reporting cycle, and any prior valuation work. From there we agree an engagement structure tailored to your fund — no standard packages imposed from the outset.

02 — Onboarding

The framework is built
before the first deadline.

Detailed review of each investment — credit agreements, IC memos, and supporting documentation. We establish our baseline valuation framework for each position so that quarterly work is rigorous and efficient from day one.

03 — Quarterly valuation

Consistent, defensible valuations
every quarter.

We review portfolio developments and deliver valuation reports for each investment. Every assumption is documented and built to withstand auditor scrutiny — without last-minute revisions or ambiguous methodology.

04 — Annual valuation report

Comprehensive coverage
when it matters most.

A full audit-ready report covering detailed analysis and careful treatment of judgemental areas. Structured to satisfy LP requirements and support a clean sign-off — including direct auditor liaison where required.
